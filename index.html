About Us

The ASME student chapter at Fr. Conceicao Rodrigues College of Engineering (CRCE), Bandra (W) is an undergraduate club of students majoring in various fields of engineering. We promote art, science, and practice of multidisciplinary engineering and allied sciences. ASME CRCE is the only International Production Council in FR. CRCE. We here at ASME believe in commitment, perseverance and backbreaking work. Our past events in the annual tech fest 'Crescendo' were a major success. We will be organizing seminars, technical paper presentations, workshops, symposiums and other professional development courses.

Our Mission

To promote and enhance the technical competency and professional well being of our members.

Founded

Under the guidance Of Prof. Sudhakar, founded by Gayatri Tandel with the help of Rounak Manghnani and Diwakar Prasad at Fr. Conceicao Rodrigues College of Engineering, Bandra (W)
